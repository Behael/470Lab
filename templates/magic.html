Magic the Gathering is a trading card game that has been around since 1993 and one of the biggest games out there on the market.

There are various types of formats that you can play Magic, each with its' own rules and restrictions that players have to build their deck under.

For beginners, I would recommend you get into the "Commander" format---the enviroment for the format ranges greatly from newbie-friendly to competetive and it was created by the players themsleves!


You can get into this format for little as $10, but be warned, the more you play, the more you'll likely get drawn into the game and thus more money spend.


Despite the warning, the game is extremely fun to play with friends, as the main purpose is to get people together and play.


There are ton of websites and forums to draw information and help from.


For starters, Magic has 5 Colours: White, Black, Red, Blue and Green---each representing a different 'aspect' that we as humans can relate to. A deck can contain any combination of colours.


Funnily enough, there are even surveys that can find out what your preferred colours are without even playing!
